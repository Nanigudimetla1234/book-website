About The Book

This book is designed as a companion for your journey into the world of careers within the pharmaceutical industry. Each chapter focuses on a unique role, acting as an individual gateway into the diverse opportunities available.
The author, SVS Guptha Mulukuri, encourages readers to approach this book not as a cover-to-cover read, but rather as a resource to revisit time and again. By opening to any page, you can immerse yourself in the story of a different expert, allowing their unique perspective and wisdom to unfold before you.
Every chapter offers a self-contained exploration of a specific role, complete with its own insights and context. The author has incorporated subtle fantasy elements to capture your imagination and connection to the content. As you read, take a moment to reflect - consider how each path aligns with your interests and jot down any insights that inspire you.
Whether you are actively exploring career options or simply seeking a glimpse into the possibilities within the pharmaceutical field, this book aims to be your guide. Dip into these stories when you feel motivated to expand your knowledge and understanding of the industry. Allow this book to reveal the multitude of rewarding journeys waiting to be discovered.

Author

SVS Guptha Mulukuri is a dedicated professional with over 14 years of experience in the pharmaceutical industry. With a passion for driving innovation and bolstering organizational excellence, he has consistently contributed to transformative projects and impactful initiatives throughout his career.
As an Associate Director of Innovation, he drives major projects, fosters a culture of innovation, and identifies industry trends to enhance organizational success. Guptha's career spans roles in pharmacovigilance, compliance, process improvements and innovation, where he has contributed to operational excellence and regulatory adherence. Recognized for his achievements, he has received awards for innovation, collaboration, and outstanding performance
In his debut book,"The Next Move: Inspiring Pharma Careers You Never Knew Existed", Guptha continues his expertise and storytelling to illuminate unconventional career paths, offering readers a unique perspective on possibilities beyond traditional roles. With his deep industry knowledge and passion for empowering others, Guptha aims to guide readers in discovering the diverse opportunities awaiting them within the pharmaceutical field.
